"SOMBRAS" is the new single by ETT, available on all streaming platforms starting Thursday, April 17th.

 

Born almost as a playful experiment, "SOMBRAS" ("shadows" in Spanish) is an evocative track that blends surreal imagery with hypnotic atmospheres, narrating a journey that is not physical but mental. The song unfolds through the streets and coffee shops of Amsterdam, transporting the listener into a world suspended between dream and reality.

 

The lyrics, written by ETT, alternate between Italian and Spanish, creating a connection between the two worlds the artist has experienced and to which she is deeply tied.

 

Composed together with dNoise, "SOMBRAS" represents the natural evolution of the electronic sounds already explored in "FRANKIE" and adds another page to the new musical chapter opened with "PSICHE", the artist's previous single.

ETT is the musical project of Gaya Misrachi, a singer-songwriter from Trieste, Italy, with Greek origins, who blends dream pop and electronic sounds with more instrumental elements, such as guitars, evoking Mediterranean atmospheres and Spanish influences. The artistic direction of the project is overseen by the artist herself, alongside her producer dNoise.

 

Her musical journey began in 2014 with Delta V, with whom she collaborated until 2016. From 2017 to 2020, she moved to Granada, where she studied at a jazz academy and performed with eight different ensembles.

 

Upon returning to Italy, she started working with Big Fish in 2021 and participated in the MI MANCHI festival. In 2023, she joined the roster of Island Records (Universal Music Italia) and RC Waves management, performing on stages such as Dr. Martens Fest (EX Macello), Germi, and Circolo Magnolia.

 

In 2024, she released her debut EP "KITSUNE", preceded by the singles "PUGNI NELLO STOMACO" and "IL TEMPO NON PASSAVA MAI", followed by "FRANKIE", "PSICHE", and "SOMBRAS".
